Privacy Policy

Bulk Codes Gen

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

Overview

Bulk Codes Gen ("the App") is a Canva app developed by Naveen Bhavnani that helps users generate unique codes in bulk for coupons, tickets, certificates, and more.

Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information or transmit user data to external servers.

The App operates entirely within the Canva platform and:

  • Does not require user login or authentication
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not track user behavior or analytics
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

Local Storage

The App uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences:

  • What is stored: Your preferred mode (random/sequential) and last used patterns
  • Where it's stored: Only on your local device, in your browser
  • Purpose: To restore your settings when you return to the App
  • How to clear: Clear your browser's local storage or site data

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

How the App Works

All code generation happens:

  • Locally in your web browser
  • Within the Canva platform environment
  • Without sending any data to external servers

Data Processing

The patterns and settings you enter into the App are:

  • Processed only to generate the codes
  • Stored only in your browser's local storage (preferences only)
  • Never transmitted outside of Canva
  • Generated codes are not stored after you close the App

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Generated Codes

When you generate codes:

  • Codes are created locally in your browser
  • No record of generated codes is kept by the App
  • You are responsible for managing and storing your generated codes
  • We have no way to retrieve or see codes you have generated

Canva Platform

This App runs within the Canva platform. Your use of Canva is governed by:

  • Canva's Privacy Policy
  • Canva's Terms of Use

Canva may collect data about your use of their platform according to their own privacy policy. This App does not have access to or control over Canva's data collection practices.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for business users and is not directed at children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data to external servers, there is no user data at risk from our side. Your preferences are stored locally in your browser.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our end. You have complete control over:

  • The patterns and settings you use
  • Local storage data (can be cleared via browser settings)
  • Any codes you generate

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

International Users

The App is available globally through the Canva platform. Since we do not collect any data on external servers, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.
