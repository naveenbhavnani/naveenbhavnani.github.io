Common Questions

QR & Barcode Studio

Q: My QR code isn't scanning. What should I do?

A: Make sure the QR code is printed at a minimum size of 25mm x 25mm and there's white space (quiet zone) around it. Test with multiple QR scanner apps.

Q: Can I change the QR code colors?

A: QR codes work best in high contrast (black on white). After inserting into Canva, you can adjust colors, but ensure sufficient contrast for reliable scanning.

Q: Is my payment information stored?

A: No. The app generates QR codes locally in your browser. No payment information is transmitted or stored anywhere.
