Privacy Policy

Color Swapper

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Overview

Color Swapper ("the App") is a Canva app developed by Naveen Bhavnani that helps users scan and swap multiple colors at once in their Canva designs.

Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information or user data.

The App operates entirely within the Canva platform and:

  • Does not require user login or authentication
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not track user behavior or analytics
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not store any data about color swaps or design modifications
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

How the App Works

All color scanning and swapping happens:

  • Locally in your web browser
  • Within the Canva platform environment
  • Using the official Canva Design API
  • Without sending any data to external servers

Data Processing

The color information the App processes (hex codes from your design) is:

  • Read directly from your Canva design using the Canva API
  • Processed only to display and apply color swaps
  • Never stored by the App
  • Never transmitted outside of Canva
  • Automatically discarded when you close the App

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Canva Platform

This App runs within the Canva platform. Your use of Canva (the platform) is governed by:

  • Canva's Privacy Policy
  • Canva's Terms of Use

Canva may collect data about your use of their platform according to their own privacy policy. This App does not have access to or control over Canva's data collection practices.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general users and is not directed at children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no user data at risk. The App processes all information locally in your browser session.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over any information you enter into the App, as it exists only in your browser session.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

International Users

The App is available globally through the Canva platform. Since we do not collect any data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.
